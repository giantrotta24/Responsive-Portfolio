About Me

Born in Venezuela, I immigrated to the United States with my family in 2001.

In the year 2010, as an amatuer musician I decided to relocate from Florida to Ohio in pursuit of opportunity. The following four years I was the guitarist for local act Strangers to Wolves.

After being introduced to the restaurant industry, I dreamed of one day owning my own. Most recently, I have been working on my web development skills by enrolling in the Case Western Reserve Web Development Boot Camp.

In my down time, I am the co-host and producer for local podcast The Kennel, A Cleveland Football Podcast.
